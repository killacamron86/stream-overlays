📋 HOW TO PLAY — ALL COMMANDS
STARTING A FIGHT
!fight @user
Challenge someone. Both players pick a fighter in 30 seconds.
!fight @user auto
Skip character select — random fighters assigned instantly.
!fight @user mirror
Both players use the same random character. Pure skill test.
!accept
Accept an incoming challenge within 60 seconds.
!decline
Decline a challenge. No penalty.
PICKING YOUR FIGHTER
!pick [name]
Choose your character during the 30 second window. Example: !pick goku
!random
Let the system pick a random character for you.
If you don't pick in time, a character is auto-assigned for you.
FIGHT MOVES (15 second window each round)
!light
Fast attack. Always lands. Low damage (6–14).
!heavy
Slow attack. High damage (18–32). Can miss 20% of the time.
!special
Signature move. Very high damage (28–44). Needs 30% meter.
!super
Ultimate attack (55–90 dmg). Needs FULL meter. Character ability activates.
!taunt
No damage. Builds your super meter +25%. Reduces enemy meter.
CHAMPIONSHIP BELT
!challenge
Challenge the current FNF Champion. They must accept within 60 seconds or forfeit the title.
!belt
See who the current champion is and their defense count.
The first fight of the night crowns the champion. Hold the belt as long as you can defend it.
COINS & BETTING
!bet @user [coins]
Bet on a fighter during any active fight. Min 10 coins. Win = 2× back.
!coins
Check your FNF coin balance.
Earn coins: watch stream (+5 per 5 min) · win fights (+150) · correct bets (2×).
INFO COMMANDS
!roster
See all available fighters in chat.
!vaulttime
Check how long the Hybrid class has been in the vault.
!fnfhelp
Quick command list posted in chat.
